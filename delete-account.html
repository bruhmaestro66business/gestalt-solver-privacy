The fastest way to delete your account and data:

  • Open Gestalt Solver

Request via email

If you no longer have access to the app, send us a deletion request from the email address associated with your account:

✉️ Send Deletion Request

We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation to your email.

🗑 What will be deleted:
  • Your email address and account credentials
  • All tasks and open-loop records
  • All emotional state entries
  • Subscription history (via RevenueCat)
  • All other data associated with your account
⏱ Deletion timeline Your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days of your request. Anonymized, non-identifiable analytics data may be retained for service improvement.
