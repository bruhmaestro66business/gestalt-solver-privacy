Privacy Policy

Gestalt Solver
Effective Date: June 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Gestalt Solver ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile productivity application that helps users plan and process open tasks and unresolved emotional states. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our app.

By using Gestalt Solver, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address — collected when you register or sign in via email.

2.2 User-Generated Content

  • Tasks, notes, and records of unresolved emotional states that you enter into the app.
  • This content is processed by AI to provide suggestions and analysis (see Section 5).

2.3 Usage and Technical Data

  • Device type, operating system version, app version, and session data collected via analytics tools.
  • Advertising identifiers for personalized ads (see Section 5).
  • Purchase history and subscription status managed via RevenueCat.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your user account.
  • To provide core app functionality: storing and organizing your tasks and emotional records.
  • To deliver AI-powered analysis and suggestions based on your entries.
  • To process payments and manage premium subscriptions.
  • To display advertisements to free-tier users.
  • To analyze app usage and improve features.
  • To respond to your support requests or inquiries.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your account data and user-generated content (tasks, emotional records) are stored on secure cloud servers provided by Supabase. Supabase infrastructure uses industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. However, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each of which may process your data according to their own privacy policies:

5.1 Supabase (Database & Backend)

Stores your account information and user-generated content.
Privacy policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

5.2 Google AdMob (Advertising)

Displays ads to free-tier users. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. Users may opt out of personalized advertising in their device settings.
Privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5.3 RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

Manages in-app purchases and premium subscriptions. RevenueCat processes purchase history and device identifiers.
Privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

5.4 Lovable AI Gateway (AI Processing)

Your user-generated content (task descriptions and emotional records) may be transmitted to AI models via Lovable AI Gateway to generate suggestions and analysis. We do not use your content to train AI models. Please do not include highly sensitive personal information (e.g., financial data, government IDs) in your entries.

6. Advertising

Gestalt Solver contains advertisements served by Google AdMob. The app is labeled as "Contains ads" on the Google Play Store. Ad-free access is available through a premium subscription. Users can manage ad personalization preferences in their device settings under Google > Ads.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share your data only:

  • With the third-party service providers listed in Section 5, to the extent necessary to operate the app.
  • When required by applicable law, court order, or government authority.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.

  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
